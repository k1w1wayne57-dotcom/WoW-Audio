About WoW Audio

WoW Audio (World of Wayne) is a personal reference database documenting Sansui audio equipment for research and second-hand market analysis. Data is sourced from audio-database.com, HiFi Engine, sansui.us, AudioKarma, and observed second-hand market listings. Pricing is conservative and based on actual sale/listing data where possible — fields are marked null rather than guessed when unverified. Every entry is flagged unverified until manually confirmed.

WoW Audio — World of Wayne. Personal reference database. Not affiliated with Sansui Electric Co.
